Bhimeswara Swamy Temple

Pancharama Kshetram: Draksharamam is one of the five sacred Pancharama Shiva temples.Mythology: Legend says Lord Shiva’s lingam here was installed by Surya Deva and is believed to be part of the shattered Atma Linga of demon Tarakasura, making it highly powerful.

The present stone structure, showcasing Chalukya architecture, dates back to the 9th–10th century CE.

Darshan Timings

Morning: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Evening: 3:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Special poojas and Abhishekam occur in the early mornings.

Best Time to Visit October to March offers pleasant weather. Major festivals: Mahashivaratri and Karthika Masam.
