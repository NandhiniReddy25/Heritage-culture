Someswara Temple

Sacred Significance: Penugonda Someswara Swamy Temple is one of the Pancharama Kshetras, where Lord Shiva is worshipped as Someswara.

Mythology: According to legend, the Lingam here was formed from a fragment of the powerful Atma Lingam shattered by Lord Kartikeya after the slaying of demon Tarakasura.

The temple exhibits Chalukya architectural style, with inscriptions dating back to the 10th–11th century CE.

Darshan Timings

Morning: 6:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Evening: 4:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Special poojas and Rudrabhishekam are popular among devotees.

Best Time to Visit October to March offers the most pleasant weather. Key festivals: Maha Shivaratri, Karthika Masam, and Sankranti.
