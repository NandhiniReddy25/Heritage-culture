Kanaka Durga Temple

Ancient Origins: The temple is dedicated to Lord Narasimha (Varaha Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy) and is one of India’s 32 Narasimha Kshetras.Legend: According to mythology, Lord Vishnu appeared here as Varaha Narasimha to save Prahlada and destroy the demon Hiranyakashipu.

The temple architecture blends Kalinga and Chola styles, with inscriptions from the 11th century, and patronage from Chola and Gajapati kings.

Darshan Timings Temple Open: 4:00 AM – 9:00 PM Key Sevas/Poojas: Suprabhata Seva: 4:00 AM Abhishekam: 6:00 AM – 8:00 AM Darshan: 7:00 AM – 8:30 PM (breaks for rituals)

Best Time to Visit October to March: Pleasant climate for sightseeing. Chandanotsavam Festival (April/May) is the most famous, when the deity’s sandalwood paste covering is removed for darshan.
