Dwaraka Tirumala Temple

Sacred Significance: Popularly known as Chinna Tirupati, this temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara (Balaji).

Legend: Sage Dwaraka is believed to have performed severe penance here to bring Lord Venkateswara to bless devotees, hence the name Dwaraka Tirumala.

Ancient inscriptions indicate that the temple dates back over 1,000 years, with contributions from the Chola and Vijayanagara dynasties.

Darshan Timings

Morning: 4:00 AM – 1:00 PM

Evening: 3:00 PM – 9:30 PM

Suprabhatam & Archana: Early morning hours. Darshan may extend during festivals and weekends.

Best Time to Visit October to March for pleasant weather. Important festivals: Brahmotsavams, Vaikunta Ekadasi, and Tirunalla (annual fair).
