Tirumala Venkateswara Temple

Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, located in Andhra Pradesh, is one of the most famous and holiest temples in India. Dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, it attracts millions of devotees every year.

The temple is known for its grand architecture, spiritual significance, and traditions. The best time to visit is during Brahmotsavam when the entire temple town comes alive with rituals and celebrations.

Months → November to February (pleasant weather, less rush).

Timings → Early morning (3 AM–7 AM) or late night (9 PM–12 AM), weekdays preferred.
