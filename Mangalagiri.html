Mangalagiri Temple

Ancient Legend: The temple is dedicated to Lord Narasimha, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu.

The deity is called Panakala Narasimha because devotees offer jaggery water (panakam) directly into the Lord’s mouth—a unique ritual said to produce a gurgling sound as if He is drinking it.

References date back to the Satya Yuga and inscriptions show patronage by the Vijayanagara kings.

Darshan Timings

Morning: 7:00 AM – 1:00 PM

Evening: 4:00 PM – 8:30 PM

Panakam Offering: Daily, especially auspicious on Saturdays and during Narasimha Jayanti.

Best Time to Visit

October to March for comfortable weather. Major festivals: Sri Narasimha Jayanti and Brahmotsavam (March–April).
