Kanaka Durga Temple

The Sri Kanaka Durga Temple is dedicated to Goddess Kanaka Durga, an incarnation of Goddess Parvati.The temple stands on Indrakeeladri Hill overlooking the Krishna River.Legends say the sage Indrakila prayed for the Goddess to reside here to protect devotees from demons.

The temple’s current form dates back to the 8th century, with later contributions from the Vijayanagara kings.

Darshan Timings Morning: 4:00 AM – 1:00 PM Evening: 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM (Timings may extend during festivals like Dasara.)

Best Time to Visit October to March is ideal for pleasant weather. Navaratri/Dasara festival (Sep–Oct) is the grandest celebration.
