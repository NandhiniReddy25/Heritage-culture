annavaram Temple

The hill-top shrine is on Ratnagiri Hill, overlooking the Pampa River and Bay of Bengal. Legend says the deity Lord Satyanarayana (a form of Lord Vishnu) manifested here to bless devotees seeking prosperity and peace.

The temple’s unique Trikoota architecture enshrines Satyanarayana Swamy, Ananta Lakshmi, and Lord Shiva in one complex. Built in the early 20th century; the famous Satyanarayana Vratam originated here and is performed daily by thousands.

Darshan Timings Morning: 4:00 AM – 1:00 PM Evening: 4:00 PM – 9:00 PM (Timings may extend during festivals like Dasara.)

Best Time to Visit October – March for pleasant weather. Major festivals: Karthika Masam, Vaikuntha Ekadasi, and Sankranti draw huge crowds.
