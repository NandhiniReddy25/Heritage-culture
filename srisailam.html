Srisailam mallikarjuna temple

Jyotirlinga & Shakti Peetha: One of the 12 Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva and one of the 18 Shakti Peethas, making it doubly sacred.

Legends: The temple is linked to the story of Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati visiting the Srisailam hills to bless devotees.

Architecture: The present stone temple, with magnificent gopurams and intricate carvings, dates back to the 7th–8th century CE, patronized by the Chalukya and Vijayanagara kings.

Timings Temple Open: 4:30 AM – 10:00 PM Key Poojas: Suprabhata Seva: 5:00 AM Abhishekam: 6:30 AM–1:00 PM & 6:30 PM–9:00 PM

Best Time to Visit October to March: Pleasant weather. Maha Shivaratri is the grandest festival, drawing lakhs of devotees.
